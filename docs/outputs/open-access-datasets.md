# All eReefs Datasets

*eReefs* is funded by the [Australian Government's Reef Trust](/about/funding#australian-government-reef-trust), and the the data that the eReefs models generate belongs to the Australian people.

All the datasets generated by the *eReefs* modelling systems are licensed as [Creative Commons by Attribution (CC-BY 4.0)](https://creativecommons.org/licenses/by/4.0/legalcode), and may be freely referenced by other data products.
